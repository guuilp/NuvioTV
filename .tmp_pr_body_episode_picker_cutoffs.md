## Summary

Fixes clipping at the first/last items in the episode picker by adding content padding to the seasons row and episodes list.

## PR type

- Bug fix

## Why

Users reported the episode picker cards were partially cut off at the edges. Adding list padding keeps the focus ring and text fully visible.
